Some computer science topics I am interested in:

  • Type systems: how can we check if programs exhibit "well-defined" behavior (type checking)? Given a program, how do we determine what the types of its expressions are (type inference)?
  • Automated formal specification and verification: can we create tools to automatically prove that a program is completely free of bugs? How do we even create specifications--by hand or by generating them?
  • Compilers and runtimes: how do we execute programming languages efficiently? How do we transform programs to become more efficient?
  • Functional programming: what are more elegant ways to write programs? How do we reap the benefits of mathematical thinking when programming?
